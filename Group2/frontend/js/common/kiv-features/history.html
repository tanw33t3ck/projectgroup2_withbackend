Travel With Us

Powered by the love of travel

"We are a company of a few, but we are strong." That is what Mr Allan Ang, CEO of Travel With Us, believed in.

Travel With Us is a boutique travel agency founded by 3 friends, Mr Allan Ang, Mr Joshua Ho and Mr Tan Wee Teck. They were brought together by their love for travel and adventure but saw a lack of reliable and quality travel agency services in Singapore. They decided to "do it themselves" and Travel With Us was born.

Travel With Us aims to provide high quality international travel packages at an affordable price. As a boutique travel agency, we specialise in group travels to Japan, USA and Europe, with a focus on Tokyo, San Franciso and London.
